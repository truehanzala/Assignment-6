Gulshion Branch

Our Gulshan branch of Jhakaas Biryani City extends the vibrant culinary tradition of Karachi's most loved biryani to the bustling heart of Gulshan-e-Iqbal. We've meticulously designed this location to provide a comfortable and inviting atmosphere, perfect for families and friends to gather and savor the authentic flavors that define our brand. Committed to the same high standards as our other locations, our Gulshan team uses fresh, quality ingredients and time-honored recipes, ensuring each plate of biryani delivers the genuine taste of Karachi. We're thrilled to be a part of the Gulshan community, offering a delicious escape and a taste of home in this lively neighborhood.
