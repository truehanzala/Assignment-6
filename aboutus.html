About Us

Welcome to Jhakaas Biryani City, Karachi's vibrant heart of authentic biryani mastery. We're no t just serving food; we're crafting an experience, a journey into the soul of Karachi's culinary heritage. Our story began with a simple yet passionate desire: to share the true essence of biryani, the kind that stirs memories and ignites the senses. We believe that biryani is more than just a dish; it's a celebration, a cultural icon, and a testament to the rich tapestry of flavors that define our beloved city. From the moment you step into Jhakaas Biryani City, you'll be enveloped in the aromatic embrace of spices and the lively buzz of a true Karachi eatery. Our open kitchen, a stage for our seasoned chefs, showcases the artistry behind each pot of biryani, a symphony of sizzling ingredients and fragrant steam. We 're committed to using time-honored techniques, passed down through generations, to create biryani that is both classic and unforgettable. Our menu is a tribute to the diverse regional variations of biryani, from the richly layered Sindhi Biryani to the delicately spiced Memoni Biryani, each prepared with unwavering dedication to authenticity. We also offer our own unique Jhakaas specials, innovative creations that push the boundaries of flavor while staying true to the heart of biryani. Beyond the biryani itself, we offer a range of traditional sides and accompaniments, from tangy raitas to flavorful chutneys, designed to complement and enhance the biryani experience.
